Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and digital wallets through our secure payment gateway. International payments are supported.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes, you can change your plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately, and for downgrades, changes will apply at the next billing cycle.

Is there a free trial?

We offer a limited free tier with basic features. You can start creating videos immediately after signing up with your Google account.

What happens if I exceed my monthly limit?

If you reach your monthly generation limit, you can upgrade your plan or wait until the next billing cycle. We'll notify you when you're approaching your limit.

Do you offer refunds?

Please refer to our Refund Policy for detailed information about our no-refund policy and the reasons behind it.
