Cancellation & Refund Policy

Last updated: November 2024

1. No Refund Policy

PixVid Pro operates under a strict no-refund policy. Once payment is processed for any subscription plan, no refunds will be provided under any circumstances.

2. Reasoning Behind Our Policy

Our no-refund policy exists for the following reasons:

2.1 Digital Service Nature

  • PixVid Pro is a digital service that provides immediate access to AI video generation capabilities
  • Once you access and use the service, the digital value has been delivered
  • Unlike physical products, digital services cannot be "returned" in a traditional sense

2.2 AI Processing Costs

  • AI video generation requires significant computational resources
  • Each video generation incurs real costs for processing power and infrastructure
  • These costs are incurred immediately when you use the service

2.3 Free Trial Availability

  • We provide access to basic features without payment
  • Users can evaluate the service quality before committing to a paid plan
  • This allows informed decision-making about subscription purchases

2.4 Clear Pricing and Features

  • All plan features and limitations are clearly stated on our Pricing page
  • Monthly limits and capabilities are transparently communicated
  • Users can review all terms before making a purchase decision

3. Subscription Cancellation

While we do not provide refunds, you can cancel your subscription at any time:

3.1 How to Cancel

  • Log into your PixVid Pro account
  • Go to your account settings or dashboard
  • Select "Cancel Subscription" option
  • Alternatively, contact our support team through the Contact page

3.2 Cancellation Effects

  • Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period
  • You retain access to paid features until the end of the paid period
  • No partial refunds are provided for unused days/months
  • Your account reverts to the free tier after cancellation

4. Payment Failures and Disputes

4.1 Failed Payments

  • If a payment fails due to technical issues, we will attempt to resolve the payment
  • No service is provided until payment is successfully processed
  • Failed payments do not constitute grounds for refunds

4.2 Unauthorized Transactions

  • Report unauthorized transactions immediately through our Contact page
  • We will investigate legitimate claims of unauthorized access
  • You are responsible for securing your Google account credentials
  • We may provide account credits in cases of proven unauthorized access

4.3 Chargebacks

  • Initiating a chargeback without contacting us first may result in account termination
  • We will contest illegitimate chargebacks
  • Please contact our support team to resolve any billing issues before involving your bank

5. Service Issues and Compensation

5.1 Service Outages

  • Temporary service outages do not qualify for refunds
  • We strive for high availability but cannot guarantee 100% uptime
  • Planned maintenance is communicated in advance when possible

5.2 Quality Issues

  • AI-generated content quality may vary based on input and current model capabilities
  • We continuously improve our AI models but cannot guarantee specific output quality
  • Users can regenerate content within their plan limits if unsatisfied with results

5.3 Account Credits

In exceptional circumstances, we may provide account credits for:

  • Extended service outages (24+ hours)
  • Proven technical issues preventing service use
  • Billing errors on our part

Credits are provided at our sole discretion and do not constitute refunds.

6. Plan Changes

  • Upgrades: Take effect immediately with prorated billing adjustments
  • Downgrades: Take effect at the next billing cycle
  • No refunds are provided for plan changes
  • Unused features from higher plans are forfeited upon downgrade

7. Legal Compliance

This refund policy is designed to:

  • Comply with applicable consumer protection laws
  • Clearly communicate our terms before purchase
  • Provide fair notice of our no-refund stance
  • Offer alternative solutions for customer concerns

8. Alternative Solutions

Instead of refunds, we offer:

  • Technical Support: Help resolve any service issues
  • Plan Adjustments: Change plans to better suit your needs
  • Usage Guidance: Tips and training on how to get better results
  • Feature Requests: Input on future service improvements

9. Contact for Issues

If you experience any issues with our service:

  1. Contact our support team through the Contact page
  2. Provide detailed description of the issue
  3. Include your account email and relevant transaction details
  4. Allow us to investigate and provide a solution

We are committed to resolving legitimate concerns through alternative means while maintaining our no-refund policy.

10. Policy Updates

  • This refund policy may be updated from time to time
  • Changes will be communicated through our website and service
  • Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy
  • The policy in effect at the time of purchase governs that transaction
